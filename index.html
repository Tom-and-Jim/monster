Monster Survivors

Dive into an intense survival shooter – eliminate endless waves of monsters and stay alive as long as you can!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced, top-down survival shooter where your goal is to last as long as possible against relentless monster hordes. Collect power-ups, upgrade your weapons, and improve your tactics to survive the nightmarish waves.

How to Play

  • Use keyboard or mouse to move and shoot
  • Collect coins and upgrade your weapons
  • Survive as long as you can against increasing monster waves

Game Features

  • Fast-paced survival gameplay
  • Intuitive controls and clean UI
  • Upgradable weapons and power-ups
  • Playable directly in browser – no download needed

Compatibility

This game is fully compatible with modern browsers on both PC and mobile devices. Best experienced in fullscreen on desktop.
